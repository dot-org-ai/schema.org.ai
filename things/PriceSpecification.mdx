# PriceSpecification

A structured value representing a price or price range. Typically, only the subclasses of this type are used for markup. It is recommended to use [[MonetaryAmount]] to describe independent amounts of money such as a salary, credit card limits, etc.

## Type Hierarchy

**PriceSpecification**

## Direct Subclasses

- **[CompoundPriceSpecification](CompoundPriceSpecification.mdx)**: A compound price specification is one that bundles multiple prices that all apply in combination for different dimensions of consumption
- **[DeliveryChargeSpecification](DeliveryChargeSpecification.mdx)**: The price for the delivery of an offer using a particular delivery method
- **[PaymentChargeSpecification](PaymentChargeSpecification.mdx)**: The costs of settling the payment using a particular payment method
- **[UnitPriceSpecification](UnitPriceSpecification.mdx)**: The price asked for a given offer by the respective organization or person
