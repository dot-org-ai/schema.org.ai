# Article

An article, such as a news article or piece of investigative report. Newspapers and magazines have articles of many different types and this is intended to cover them all.\
\
See also [blog post](https://blog.schema.org/2014/09/02/schema-org-support-for-bibliographic-relationships-and-periodicals/).

## Type Hierarchy

[Thing](Thing.mdx) > [CreativeWork](CreativeWork.mdx) > **Article**

## Direct Subclasses

- **[AdvertiserContentArticle](AdvertiserContentArticle.mdx)**: An [[Article]] that an external entity has paid to place or to produce to its specifications
- **[NewsArticle](NewsArticle.mdx)**: A NewsArticle is an article whose content reports news, or provides background context and supporting materials for understanding the news
- **[Report](Report.mdx)**: A Report generated by governmental or non-governmental organization
- **[SatiricalArticle](SatiricalArticle.mdx)**: An [[Article]] whose content is primarily [[satirical]](https://en
- **[ScholarlyArticle](ScholarlyArticle.mdx)**: A scholarly article
- **[SocialMediaPosting](SocialMediaPosting.mdx)**: A post to a social media platform, including blog posts, tweets, Facebook posts, etc
- **[TechArticle](TechArticle.mdx)**: A technical article - Example: How-to (task) topics, step-by-step, procedural troubleshooting, specifications, etc
